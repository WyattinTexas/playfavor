FAVOR — Support

Corkscrew Games

FAVOR is a digital edition of the Corkscrew Games tabletop card game — draft cards, complete missions, win melees, and earn the Queen's Favor across three acts. Play at playfavor.net or in the app editions.

Common questions

Do I need an account? No. A royal identity is minted for you the first time you play, and it persists on your device.

How does multiplayer work? Hitting Play Now pledges you to a match. If other players are queued for your table size, you're matched live; otherwise the realm fills your table so the game always starts.

How do I learn the rules? Tap How to Play on the title screen for the guided tour, and keep an eye on the coach tips during your first game.

My stars or rating look wrong. Email us with your royal alias and what you expected — we'll look at the ledger.
