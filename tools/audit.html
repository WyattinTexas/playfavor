Findings on the two reported bugs (7/22):
  • Great North Connection + 18 Gold: the card grants NO gold (data and engine both: 5 Favor + trade-route borrowing + a map). The only mechanic that pays you gold on another player's turn is lending: 2 Gold per skill borrowed from you. 18 Gold = 9 skills borrowed — most likely the next player borrowed heavily for a big card or mission. Working as designed, but 9-skill borrows may deserve a design cap.
  • Magician +1 Power at melee (ring at center): the Magician board has NO Power in any slot, either side — verified in data. The melee's "Board" tile is a catch-all: it wears ALL power not attributable to a played card or mission. The likely true source: Side B's 4th-slot pick-one includes Power, and a picked skill is permanent — it stays after the ring moves away. So the +1 was probably real, just mislabeled as "the board." Recommended fix: record slot picks so the melee can caption them honestly.
